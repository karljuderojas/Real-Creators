Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 28, 2026

Verified People ("the Extension") is a Chrome browser extension that labels creator accounts on X (Twitter) as verified human creators or AI creators. This policy explains what data the Extension collects, why, and how it is handled.

1. Data We Collect

The Extension collects the following anonymous data:

  • Anonymous session ID. A randomly generated identifier (UUID) is created when you first install the Extension and stored locally on your device. It is never linked to your name, email, X account, or any personally identifiable information.
  • Badge impressions. When a verified or AI badge is displayed on a creator profile, the Extension records which creator handle triggered the badge and the timestamp. Your session ID is included so we can understand usage patterns without identifying individuals.
  • Profile visit clicks. When you click a verified creator's profile link from inside the X feed, the Extension records the creator's handle and the timestamp.
  • Community votes. If you submit a vote on whether a creator is a real human, AI creator, or you're unsure, the Extension records the creator's handle, your vote, and your session ID.

The Extension does not collect your name, email address, X username, passwords, browsing history outside of X, or any other personal information.

2. How We Use This Data

  • Badge impressions and profile visit data help us understand which creators are most visible and how the Extension is being used.
  • Community votes are reviewed by a human moderator before any changes are made to the creator lists. No vote automatically affects what other users see.

3. Data Storage

Data is transmitted to and stored on Supabase, a third-party database service. Supabase stores data on servers located in the United States. By using the Extension you agree to Supabase's Privacy Policy.

The following data is stored locally on your device only and never sent anywhere:

  • The local cache of creator lists (refreshed every 6 hours)
  • The list of creator profiles you have already voted on (so the vote prompt doesn't reappear)

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your data with any third party, except Supabase which acts as our data processor as described above.

5. Data Retention

Analytics data (badge impressions, profile visits) and community votes are retained indefinitely for the purpose of improving the Extension and maintaining the creator lists. You may request deletion of any data associated with your session ID by contacting us at the email below.

6. Children's Privacy

The Extension is not directed at children under the age of 13 and we do not knowingly collect data from children. If you believe a child has submitted data through the Extension, please contact us and we will delete it.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page will reflect any changes. Continued use of the Extension after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
