How it works

Supabase (source of truth)

verified_creators + ai_creators tables. Centrally managed; community submissions are manually reviewed by the team before being added.

→

Background worker

Fetches both lists every 6 hours and caches in chrome.storage.local. Batches anonymous analytics (impressions, profile visits) and flushes every 60s.

→

Content script (x.com)

Reads cached handle maps, scans the DOM, and injects green/blue badges next to usernames. Debounced MutationObserver handles the SPA timeline.

→

Toolbar popup

Shows live counts, last sync time, and quick links to X/Twitter and Ko-fi support.
