Badge Definitions

What each badge means and when to use them.

Verified Creator

A creator that focuses on traditional methods and does not utilize AI in their main workflow.

AI Creator

A creator that utilizes AI in their main workflow.

Not Sure

If you are not sure whether the creator utilizes traditional methods, or AI methods in their workflow. This signals to the team that this needs further verification.

Community contributions are manually reviewed by the team before being added to the database, so there may be a delay between submission and the badge appearing for users.
